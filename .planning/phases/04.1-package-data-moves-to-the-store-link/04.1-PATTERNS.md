### `src/domain/parser.py` (service, transform)

**Analog:** itself.

**Prompt-bullet pattern** (`parser.py:216-232`) — new bullets copy this exact `- "field": description` form; the `unit_price` bullet is the one that must be rewritten (it currently *instructs the LLM to compute*, contradicting D-05):
```

- "price": Regular price in SEK as a number (e.g., 29.90), null if not found
- "unit_price": Price per piece/item for multi-packs. For products like toilet paper,
```
